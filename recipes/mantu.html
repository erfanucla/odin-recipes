Mantu!

Now this, this is where the good stuff is at. This is called mantu, and Afghan staple and traditional dish celebrated by many. It is a great lunch and dinner, and can fill any appetite for any palette. Why? Because my mothers cooking says so. Sweet, spicy, tangy, juicy, all of it. Yummy.

Ingredients

  • Ground Meat (lamb or beef)
  • Onions
  • Spices, like Cumin, Coriander, Chili Powder
  • Wonton wrappers, made of flour, salt,etc
  • Yogurt, paired with garlic & dried mint
  • Tomato-based sauce, with sprinkled greens

Steps

  1. Make the wonton wrappers by scratch!
  2. Grind up ya lamb or beef, whichever
  3. Season da meat with all spices necessary
  4. Put ya meat with diced onions
  5. Throw that shi on the wrappers, and wrap!
  6. Put in a tradish pot and simmer for 1 hour
  7. Cook up ya sauce based seasoning, and use it
