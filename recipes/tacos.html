Tacos!

Welcome to your taco tuesday backrooms. This is a classic Mexican dish known as TACOS! At its core, a taco consists of a tortilla folded around a filling, which can range from various types of meat like carne asada, carnitas, or al pastor, to seafood, beans, and vegetables. Traditional Mexican street tacos typically feature soft corn tortillas and minimal, fresh toppings like cilantro, onions, and a squeeze of lime, emphasizing the flavor of the filling.
