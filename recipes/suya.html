Suya!

Suya is a spicy meat skewer, a popular West African street food, particularly in Nigeria. It typically consists of thinly sliced meat, often beef, that is marinated in a blend of spices, skewered, and grilled over an open flame.

Ingredients

  • Yaji
  • Salt
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Stock Cubes

Steps

  1. Finely grind roasted peanuts
  2. Combine spices like ground ginger, onion p, etc
  3. Whisk thoroughly
  4. Thinly slice beef, marinate for 30 min, refridgerate
  5. Grill!
